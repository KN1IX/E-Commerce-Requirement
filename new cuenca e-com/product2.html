Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare

The game is set in a distant future where Earth's resources are depleted, leading to the formation of the United Nations Space Alliance (UNSA) and the Solar Associated Treaty Organization (SATO) to defend Earth and its colonies. Players take on the role of Nick Reyes, a pilot-turned-commander, who must lead the remnants of the UNSA forces against the fanatical Settlement Defense Front (SDF) after a devastating attack cripples the UNSA.
